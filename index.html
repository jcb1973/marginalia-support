Marginalia

Support and help for the Marginalia reading notebook app

About Marginalia

Marginalia is a private reading notebook designed for readers who want a structured way to track books, organise tags, and capture notes and quotes.

The app does not use accounts, does not include social features, and does not require a subscription. Your data remains on your device unless you choose to export it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a book?

Tap the plus button to add a new book. You can scan the ISBN barcode or enter details manually.

How does quote capture work?

Use the camera inside a book to capture text. Marginalia uses on-device OCR to extract the quote. You can then edit or comment on it.

How do tag filters work?

In the Library view, select one or more tags. You can switch between matching any selected tags or requiring all selected tags.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your library, notes, and quotes to CSV at any time.

Data & Privacy

Marginalia does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

All reading data is stored locally on your device. You may export your data at any time.
